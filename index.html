NAFDAC Directive

Antimalarial Suspension Phase-out

NAFDAC has issued an enforcement mandate ordering the phase-out of all multi-dose liquid Artemether/Lumefantrine suspensions (including bottle **Lonart Suspension** for children) due to rapid stability and efficacy loss after water dilution.

Obitex Customer Action Policy:

1. We have fully audited our shelves and do not dispense phased-out liquid bottles.
2. Parents are advised to transition pediatric patients to safer, NAFDAC-approved **dispersible tablets** (like Lonart Dispersible) which retain full malaria-fighting power.

Avoid "Lonart App" Verification Scams

Greenlife Pharmaceuticals warns that there is no official Lonart app. Always verify medications using the official Mobile Authentication Service (MAS) text shortcode.

Interactive MAS Product Validator

Scratch the silver panel on your pack, enter your 12-digit PIN below, and press verify to test the system.
